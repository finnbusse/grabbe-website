## 2024-03-04 - Native Image Elements in Lists
**Learning:** The codebase frequently uses native `<img>` tags instead of `next/image` in critical public-facing components like `NewsSection` and `AktuellesContent`. While converting them to `next/image` requires careful domain configuration and architectural decisions, a simple and highly effective first step is adding `loading="lazy"` and `decoding="async"`. Wait to optimize "featured" or above-the-fold images: they should explicitly use `loading="eager"` to protect LCP (Largest Contentful Paint).
**Action:** When finding unoptimized `<img>` tags in list views, apply `loading="lazy" decoding="async"` as a low-risk, high-reward optimization. Ensure above-the-fold images use `loading="eager"`.
